Chicago Crime Records

A collection of crime data for Chicago from 2001 to the present

Chicago Crime Records is a collection of data related to crime incidents reported through the years 2001-2018 throughout the 77 Community Areas of Chicago, Illinois. The data set contains information such as date, location, type of crime. On our Explore Data page, you can search for crimes with several filter options. Additionally, on our Location Stats page, you can take a look at how the crime rate has changed in recent years, in a specific community area. Our data was accessed from data.gov.
